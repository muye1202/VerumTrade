How a run works

VerumTrade analyzes a ticker you care about — or discovers new candidates — and turns market data, fundamentals, news, sentiment, catalysts, and risk context into an auditable trade thesis instead of a black-box buy/sell signal.

  1. 01 · Analysts
    Gather evidence
    Market, news, social, fundamental, and catalyst signals.
  2. 02 · Evidence Graph
    Structure the facts
    Findings distilled into a grounded fact graph.
  3. 03 · Debate
    Bull vs. bear
    The thesis is tested from both sides.
  4. 04 · Trader plan
    Propose an action
    The debate becomes a concrete proposal.
  5. 05 · Risk review
    Check the downside
    Sizing, timing, concentration, fragility.
  6. 06 · Decision
    Record & (optionally) execute
    Rationale, traces, and paper/live Alpaca orders.

Built on TradingAgents

VerumTrade builds on Tauric Research’s TradingAgents and extends it with an evidence graph, a catalyst/event-risk analyst, macro-pullback & crowding awareness, AI stock discovery, and visible reasoning & decision traces rendered in a web UI.
